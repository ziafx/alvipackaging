ADVANTAGES OVER BOPP/PLASTIC/PVC TAPES

Water Activated Tape (WAT)
Water activated tapes are manufactured on High Strength Virgin Kraft Paper and have a Vegetable Starch based adhesive for packaging and sealing purpose of Corrugated Boxes.

Its Greener

For eco minded customers committed to sustainability it is our recommendation to always use WAT.

More Effective

The vegetable adhesive on WAT is not only natural; it also bonds aggressively to the corrugated box, especially even if the box is under-filled.

Tamper Evidence

Gummed Paper Tape cannot be removed from the carton without leaving traces of damage or pilferage.

All Weather Use & Noiseless

WAT is not at all affected by extreme hot or cold temperatures and works well in humid and dusty conditions.

ADVANTAGES OF REINFORCED PAPER TAPES

Reinforced Paper Tapes
Re-inforced Gummed Paper Tapes ( Reinforced Temper Evident Tapes). It has two laminated Kraft Papers having layer of longitudinal and diagonally placed glass fibre threads. APIPL is the first manufacturer of Reinforced Gummed Paper Tapes in India.
  • Available in four Colors
    It can be printed in up to four colours as per the branding artwork and demand of the customer.
  • Available in five breaking lengths
    Re-inforced Gummed Tape is available in up to five Breaking lengths and in standard width of 35, 50, 70 & 100mm width & is standard length of 100 meters.
  • Tamper Proof Sealing
    It is used for packing fibreboard corrugated boxes from 2.0 to 40.0 Kg giving temper proof sealing.
